"Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix" is the fifth book in J.K. Rowling's beloved wizarding series. The story continues to follow Harry Potter, a young wizard, during his fifth year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The book begins as Harry returns to school and discovers that the wizarding community is beginning to deny the return of Voldemort's dark forces. Meanwhile, Harry also faces challenges from the new Ministry of Magic administration and a new teacher at Hogwarts. The book quickly becomes an intense adventure with surprising discoveries and gripping battles.
